Muhua Zhu

Research Scientist at Tencent AI Lab

zhumuhua@gmail.com

Bio

Muhua Zhu got his Ph.D degree from Northeastern University, China in 2013. After graduation, he worked at Nuance (2013-2014), Alibaba (2014-2016), and now he is working at Tencent as a research scientist.

Research Interests

  • Natural Language Processing (syntactic and semantic parsing)
  • Conversation Agents (chit-chat, question answering)
